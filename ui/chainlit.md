# Q&A System

Welcome to the Document Q&A System! This interface allows you to ask questions about your uploaded documents.

## How to Use

1. Simply type your question in the input field below
2. The system will search through your documents and provide grounded answers
3. Answers include citations showing which documents and pages the information came from

## Features

- **Grounded Answers**: All answers are based on your document content
- **Citation Tracking**: See exactly where information comes from
- **Conversation Memory**: The system remembers previous questions and answers
- **Automatic Summarization**: Long conversations are summarized to maintain context

## Tips

- Ask specific questions for better results
- The system will ask for clarification if it can't find relevant information
- Conversation history is automatically managed
